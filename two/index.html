About

Currently studying Computing Science at the University of Alberta.

I am interested in high frequency and algorithmic trading. I am curious to see how viable machine learning is trading equity markets. While I don't share in the enthusiam for Bitcoin, I think there are also a lot of interesting opportunities for ECN and smart routing software at the moment; though in general, I am more interested in traditional markets.

Other interests include sports analytics and it's visualization. More specifically, I am interested in the increased use of data in hockey.

Currently prototyping an application that allows users to store gym routines and view progress over time. The project started as a way to learn Flask and D3js, but has shifted to a mobile application built in Apache Cordova. End vision is an application that is simple, and lightweight, that does away with the distraction of social networks, and user accounts. Everything is stored locally on the phone.
